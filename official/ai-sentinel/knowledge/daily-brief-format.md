# Daily Brief Format

## Editorial goal

The brief should be readable in 3-5 minutes on a phone and help a busy reader answer:

- what changed
- why it matters
- what deserves action or monitoring next

## Recommended structure

```text
━━━ AI 哨兵日报 | YYYY-MM-DD ━━━

📌 编辑一句话

━━ 🔥 今日头条 ━━
【事件标题】
What's new
Why it matters
来源 + 置信度

━━ 📡 行业动态 ━━
3-4 条速览

━━ 🔬 技术前沿 ━━
1-2 条论文或开源进展

━━ 🛠️ 今日工具 ━━
一个值得关注的工具、产品或能力

━━ ⚡ 快讯 ━━
3-5 条一句话信息

━━ 📈 本周趋势雷达 ━━
加速中 / 新出现 / 减弱中
```

## Minimum fields per key item

- fact core: who / what / when
- impact core: who is affected and what decision changes
- evidence core: at least one first-party or authority source
- structure tags: entities, event type, topic tags
- confidence

## Quality gates

- structure is complete
- adjacent sections are not repetitive
- no unsupported claims
- unverified points are labeled
- the lead story contains actual consequence, not just a headline rewrite

## Writing tone

- short, decisive, traceable
- no breathless hype
- no raw worker dump
- no overloaded jargon when a concise explanation is possible
